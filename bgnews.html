Low and Slow

posted by BGB, November 15, 2016

This week I am extrmely excited about a new cooking technique called sous vide. In sous vide cooking, you submerge the food (usually vacuum-sealed in plastic) into a water bath that is percisely set to the target temperature of the food. In his book, Cooking for Geeks, Jeff Potter describes it as a ultra-low-temperature.
